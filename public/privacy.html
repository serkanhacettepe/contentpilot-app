Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

ContentPilot respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our website, waitlist, and software platform.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information such as your name and email address.
  • Account and billing-related information when you purchase a plan.
  • Usage information such as product interactions, analytics, and technical logs.
  • Content you provide while using the platform.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve ContentPilot.
  • To process purchases, subscriptions, and customer support requests.
  • To communicate product updates, access details, and service notices.
  • To monitor platform performance, security, and abuse prevention.

Payments

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers. We do not directly store full payment card details on our servers.

Waitlist and Marketing

If you join our waitlist or contact us, we may use your email to send onboarding information, product updates, and launch-related communications. You can opt out of non-essential emails at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate our website, payments, hosting, analytics, and customer support.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@getcontentpilot.com.
